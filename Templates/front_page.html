Transition from ACS Education to Heartland ECSI

If you have an institutional student loan from a University of California campus (such as a Perkins Loan, Health Professional student loan, and/or an institutional loan, but NOT a federal Direct Loan), your student loan will now be serviced by Heartland ECSI.
